MANAGING YOUR FOREIGN CURRENCY

The Bank Currency Account is an easy-to-manage currency holding account, with no monthly account fee. With accounts available in 14 major currencies, it allows you to make payments into and out of your accounts.

Designed to support your international lifestyle, with easy access to your account via Online Service, you can manage your finances wherever you are in the world.

Account features:

No account fee

There's no monthly fee for the account and no payment fee when sending money to another Bank account anywhere in the world. You can also make payments to non Bank accounts overseas fees may apply.

Live exchange rates

With live rates updated by the second during market hours, we'll provide you with our most up-to-date foreign exchange rate available when making payments.

Online access

You can easily make transfers between your Bank accounts or send money to an account abroad via Online Service.

Managing your payments

You can create immediate international payments or set these up online up to a year in advance or on a recurring basis.

Bank in the currency of your choice

Choose to hold your account in one of 14 major currencies including Euro, US Dollar and Chinese Renminbi.

Credit is subject to status, affordability and applicable terms and conditions.

Bank Currency Account

Find out how to apply for a Bank Currency Account today

How to apply for a Bank Currency Account

Eligibility:

To apply for an Bank Currency Account you need to hold an active Bank current account (excluding Basic Bank Account),

Be a resident of the country

Be 18 years of age or over

If you're not yet an Bank customer, find out more about our current accounts.

You can credit your Bank Currency Account by making a transfer from your Bank sterling account online, in branch or via the telephone. You can also pay into your account from another bank account - account details can be found on your welcome letter or on your statement. Please note: Cash deposits are not available with this account.

When accessing your account via Online Service note that between 10pm Saturday until 8am Sunday (US time) we will undertake system maintenance and can only provide limited access to your Bank Currency Account(s).

During this maintenance period, you will be able to view your account though the balances and transactions may not be up to date and it is not possible to make transfers to or from your Bank Currency Account(s) or to make payments from it.

You can withdraw money from your Bank Currency Account by making a transfer to your Bank sterling account either online, in branch or via the telephone. You can also arrange for a payment to be made out of the account using our International Payments service or cash withdrawal by visiting any of our Bank US Branches. Please note: Cash deposits are not available with this account. We also do not offer overdraft facilities on this account, therefore please ensure you have sufficient funds in your account prior to making a payment or withdrawal.

We will notify you each time you make a payment from your account and where a currency exchange has been involved, we will confirm the Bank Exchange Rate that has been applied.

There is no interest payable on the Bank Currency Account.

International payments:

Online: up to $50,000 (or currency equivalent)

Branch: unlimited

Transfers:

Transfers between your own Bank accounts are unlimited.

International Payments

How to make an international payment

Make a payment online

Log on to Online Service and:

1. Select the account from where you want to make the payment and select 'Move Money'

2. Enter payment details

3. Follow the on-screen instructions

4. Review and confirm your payment

you can also make payments by phone or in branch

Receiving money from abroad

Things you need to know before receiving a payment

The overseas CapitalGuard Financial Union sending the payment to you will need to know the following information about the account you wish to be credited – Providing this information will help to ensure that the payment happens as quickly as possible.

1. Your personal details

2. SWIFT Code

3. IBAN (International Bank Account Number) or account number

4. BIC (Business Identifier Code)

It’s possible for you to receive payments into your account from abroad in either foreign currency or Sterling.

If a payment is received in a different currency to what your account is held in, we'll convert it using the most up to date CapitalGuard Financial Union Exchange Rate available before crediting it to your account.

We'll make your international payment using the details you provide to us, so it's really important you check they are correct to avoid any unnecessary delays and / or charges.

Before you make an international payment it's important that you have the following details to hand about your beneficiary and payment:

Your account details: for the Bank Account you wish to be debited

Beneficiary's full name and address

Beneficiary's bank code: You should obtain this code, normally the Business Identifier Code (BIC), from your beneficiary. For some countries you may need the national bank code rather than the BIC.

IBAN (International Bank Account number) or account number: The IBAN is a series of alphanumeric characters that helps to identify accounts held anywhere in the world. Some beneficiaries may hold just an account number depending on their bank.

Country of their bank: Your beneficiaries' account holding bank may not always be the same as their home country

Amount of the payment

Any reference: Any reference you would like to be included to be sent to the beneficiary, however we cannot guarantee this information will be passed to your beneficiary by their bank

Reason for making the payment: It is important to provide us with this information if we ask for it

Online: up to $50,000 (or currency equivalent)

Branch: unlimited

Telephone: unlimited for Premier, and up to $10,000 (or currency equivalent) for Non-Premier

Payments to accounts in the EEA in euro and sterling will normally be credited the next working day*. All other payments will normally take up to four working days* (but may take longer depending on the country the money is being sent to).

* subject to currency cut-off time and further checks, as from time to time we may need to conduct additional security checks to validate the payment.

Currently this option is only available online when logged into Online Service.

The exchange rate applied to your instruction will be the CapitalGuard Financial Union Exchange Rate applicable at the time your payment is made. This is a live rate which is updated by the second during market hours. We will notify you each time you make a payment from your account and where a currency exchange has been involved, we will confirm the CapitalGuard Financial Union Exchange Rate that has been applied.

For future dated payments, the exchange rate will be applied on the day that the payment is made.

You can check the actual exchange rate used for the payment by viewing 'Historic payments' within 'Manage payments'.

We will also write to you to advise of the exchange rate applied to the payment where applicable.

If you've asked us to make a payment immediately you will be unable to cancel it.

You can cancel a future dated or recurring international payment set up online by selecting 'Manage payments - future payments' in Send Money Overseas via Online Service. We will need to have received your request to cancel by 23.59 (US Time) on the day before the payment is due to be made.

TRAVEL MONEY MADE EASY

At CapitalGuard Financial Union we’ve made ordering your Travel Money quick and easy – order online and choose home delivery or collect in branch.

Travel money features:

Travel money features:

You can check our competitive rates online.

Quick & easy to order online

Just follow the onscreen instructions and order your Travel Money in minutes.

All major currencies available

We offer a wide range of foreign currencies for your Travel Money needs.

Fee free next day delivery

Order online by 2pm and have your money delivered to your home or chosen branch the next working day. Until 10 December 2018 all orders will be delivered fee free. Terms and Conditions apply. Minimum order $100.

Your travel checklist

Make sure to follow these tips before, during and after you travel:

1. Contact us to let us know you're travelling and taking your debit / credit card with you, and note down the lost / stolen emergency number just in case

2. Order your Travel Money online, by phone or in branch

3. Get travel insurance1 and take the policy details along with you

4. When you return, take your unused notes back to your branch and we'll buy them back from you
