THE CAPITALGUARD CREDIT CARD

Available exclusively for existing CapitalGuard customers, this card brings you a world of travel benefits, a rewards programme and access to CapitalGuard Privileges. If you qualify, you could enjoy interest-free periods on balance transfers and purchases – all for no annual fee.

Credit is subject to status, affordability and applicable terms and conditions.

Card details

0% interest on balance transfers for 18 months* from account opening

The rate for balance transfers is 0% for 18 months, where the transfer is made within 60 days of account opening. A 1.4% balance transfer fee applies to each balance transferred (minimum $5)*.

0% interest on purchases for 9 months* from account opening

At the end of the promotional period, Balance Transfer and Purchases will revert to the standard variable rate.

No annual fee

*At the end of the promotional period, balance transfers and purchases will revert to the standard variable rate.

Benefits

Earn points when you spend

Earn 1 point for every $1 you spend in Sterling*, and 2 points for every $1 spent in other currencies. *Sterling currency includes transactions made in the country, Jersey, Guernsey and Isle of Man as well as transactions overseas where you choose to pay in Sterling rather than local currency.

CapitalGuard Rewards for Miles

Every 2 points are worth the equivalent of 1 Frequent Flyer Mile with our selected partner airlines.

Extended warranty cover

Get up to 2 years extended warranty on selected household appliances. See below for more details.

CapitalGuard Privileges

A unique collection of special discounts and experiences exclusively for our CapitalGuard Credit Card customers.

Enjoy 24/7 worldwide assistance

Day or night, help is always at hand if your card is lost or stolen. Wherever you are in the world you can speak to our dedicated team about getting an emergency cash advance.

Enjoy airport lounge access

With membership in the LoungeKey programme, just show your CapitalGuard credit card to gain access to over 750 airport lounges worldwide. Please note, lounges charge $15 per person per visit, subject to availability.

Free unlimited Wi-Fi

Take advantage of free, unlimited and secure Wi-Fi via Boingo. With over 1 million hotspots worldwide, you can connect up to four devices at airports, hotels and more. Sign up with your CapitalGuard Credit Card at http://Bankuk.boingo.com

Hotel discounts

Receive a 10% discount on selected hotels booked with your CapitalGuard Credit Card through Expedia or a 10% discount on over 180,000 hotels booked with your CapitalGuard Credit Card through www.agoda.com/BankPremier. Agoda CapitalGuard benefits. Cardholders also benefit from 12 months’ complimentary Expedia+ Gold Membership.

Get there with Uber

Receive a $20 Uber promo code every time you purchase airline ticket(s) (value of $500 and above) using your CapitalGuard Credit Card.*
