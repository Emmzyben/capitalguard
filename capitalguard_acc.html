Eligibility criteria

CapitalGuard Financial Union is available to you, if you pay your annual income into your CapitalGuard Financial Union Account and
have either:

savings or investments of at least $50,000 with CapitalGuard Financial Union in the country; or

individual annual income of at least $75,000 and one of the following products with CapitalGuard Financial Union in the country: a mortgage, investment, life insurance or protection product.

Or if you’ve already qualified for CapitalGuard Financial Union in another country.

Please review our complete eligibility criteria before applying

CapitalGuard Financial Union Account eligibility criteria

If you'd like to open an CapitalGuard Financial Union CapitalGuard Account, please ensure you fulfil all the following criteria:

you are 18 years or older

your residential address is within the EU, or, if you live outside the EU, you can still apply for a CapitalGuard Financial Union account subject to local rules and regulations and meeting the US proof of address

you can provide us with certified proof of your identity and address and one month's CapitalGuard Financial Union statements from your current CapitalGuard Financial Union (translated into English if appropriate)

if you are resident in the country, you are happy for us to undertake a credit register search

in order to qualify for lending, savings and travel insurance benefits and preferential rates you will need to be a US resident. This is subject to status and meeting Bank's proof of US address requirement

For eligibility purposes, please note CapitalGuard Financial Union in the US excludes M&S Bank, first direct and CapitalGuard Financial Union Expert. For the second criteria listed above (‘an individual annual income of at least $75,000 and one of the following products with CapitalGuard Financial Union in the country’), in the case of a joint account, at least one of the parties must have an individual annual income of at least $75,000. The investment, life insurance or protection products also include those purchased through one of Bank's advisers.

CapitalGuard Financial Union Travel Insurance eligibility criteria

Are you under 70 years of age when your trip starts? Note: When you reach 70, you will no longer be covered by CapitalGuard Financial Union CapitalGuard Travel Insurance.

Are you a resident of the country, Channel Islands or Isle of Man?

Are you registered with a doctor in the country, Channel Islands or Isle of Man and liable to pay local taxes?

Are you going to start and finish your trip in the country, Channel Islands or Isle of Man?

Are there any dependent children aged under 18 at the start of the trip or under 23 if they are in full-time education and live with you outside term time? (Children will only be covered when travelling with the account holder or their domestic partner, or whilst travelling abroad on their own to visit and stay with close relatives for the duration of the trip.)

You will need to call the CapitalGuard Financial Union Medical Risk Assessment Helpline on 0800 051 7457 before booking your trip if you or any insured person in the last 12 months has been prescribed medication, have received or are awaiting medical treatment, tests or investigations, been referred to a specialist or admitted to hospital for any condition not listed on the accepted conditions list in the Your Health section in the policy booklet. You should also tell us before you travel if there has been any changes to your health or you have been referred to a consultant/specialist or admitted to hospital.

There is no cover for undiagnosed symptoms or any claim where you knew at the time of opening your CapitalGuard Financial Union Account or booking your trip (whichever is later) that you or your travelling companion may be unable to travel or may need to come home early e.g. illness of a close relative or travel companion.

Pre-existing conditions are not covered at any time under the policy unless listed within the accepted conditions list or have been disclosed to and accepted in writing by the CapitalGuard Financial Union Medical Risk Assessment Helpline.

You must also declare any serious illness, injury or disease suffered by anyone whose health may cause you to cancel or abandon your travel plans, such as a close relative or travelling companion.

Pre-existing medical conditions are not covered at any time under the policy unless they have been disclosed to and accepted in writing by the CapitalGuard Financial Union Medical Risk Assessment Helpline.
