LIFE INSURANCE

Providing peace of mind

About capitalguard Financial Union Life Insurance

capitalguard Financial Union Life Insurance offers you peace of mind, knowing that your loved ones are financially protected if the unexpected happens. We offer Life Cover, Critical Illness Cover and Income Cover. You can find more details by clicking the links below.

Existing customers

If you need to make a claim, contact our helpline for assistance as soon as you can.


Eligibility

To apply for Life Cover online, you'll need to be an existing capitalguard Financial Union customer and registered for capitalguard Financial Union banking, and hold a capitalguard Financial Union account from which you'll make your monthly payments by direct debit.

To be eligible for capitalguard Financial Union Life Insurance products:

You must be a US resident (not including the Channel Islands or the Isle of Man).

Life Cover eligibility requirements:

17 or over and under 70 years of age

Cover must cease before your 80th birthday

Unfortunately, you can't apply for Critical Illness or Income Cover online at the moment. Before you visit your local branch it's worth checking you meet the eligibility requirements which are:

Critical Illness eligibility requirements:

17 or over and under 65 years of age

must cease before your 70th birthday

Income Cover eligibility requirements:

17 or over and under 60 years of age

Cover must cease before your 68th birthday

In paid employment for more than 16 hours a week including self employment

CAPITALGUARD FINANCIAL UNION LIFE COVER

At capitalguard Financial Union, we're here to give your loved ones the financial support they may need if the unexpected happens.

Single or Joint Life Cover available

Level Term or Decreasing Term Cover

Lump sum payable on death or diagnosis of a terminal illness up to $2,000,000

Accidental death benefit, free cover up to a maximum of $250,000

Life Cover is underwritten by capitalguard Financial Union Life (UK) Ltd. Get a personalised quotation and apply today.

CAPITALGUARD FINANCIAL UNION CRITICAL ILLNESS COVER

Our Critical Illness Cover can offer you and your loved ones financial support should you unexpectedly fall ill.

A Lump sum of up to $1,000,000 on diagnosis paid free of US income and capital gains taxes

Lump sum paid on diagnosis of one of the 35 specified Critical Illnesses or surgical procedures

Level Term or Decreasing Cover

Children's cover included

Critical Illness Cover is underwritten by capitalguard Financial Union Life (UK) Ltd. Get a personalised quotation and apply today.

CAPITALGUARD FINANCIAL UNION INCOME COVER

Our income cover pays a monthly tax-free benefit to help replace your loss of earnings if you're unable to work due to an accident or injury.

Monthly benefit paid directly to your capitalguard Financial Union account

Choose between benefits that pay while you are unable to work for up to 5 years, or continue to your 68th birthday

Dedicated claims assessor is on hand to offer support

Premiums for any capitalguard Financial Union Life and Protection products you hold are waived while you receive Income Cover benefits

Income Cover is underwritten by capitalguard Financial Union Life (UK) Ltd. Get a personalised quotation and apply today.
