CAPITALGUARD FINANCIAL ADVICE SERVICE

Financial Advice Service

Whether your dream is to retire in style, manage and grow your wealth or provide financial security for the future of your loved ones, with the help of your dedicated capitalguard Relationship Manager, a qualified financial adviser, you could start to build the future you’ve imagined.



capitalguard Financial Union capitalguard Financial Advice Service is only available to US residents who have $50,000 or more in savings and investments and who are at least 18 years old.

You'll also need an capitalguard Financial Union or First Direct current account or savings account* for us to be able to deduct your fee. * Excluding capitalguard Financial Union Online Bonus Saver, capitalguard Financial Union Online Saver, any capitalguard Financial Union or First Direct Cash ISA, First Direct E-Savings or First Direct Everyday Saver.

If you don't qualify for capitalguard Financial Union capitalguard Financial Advice Service, or if you'd prefer not to pay for advice, see other ways we can help.

If you are not a US resident, see our capitalguard Financial Union Expat service.

We charge for our service - our fees vary depending on your individual needs. Your adviser will clearly explain our fees upfront and the type of advice we offer in your no-obligation discussion.

capitalguard Financial Union capitalguard Financial Advice Service is provided by capitalguard Financial Union plc. We have chosen to offer restricted advice. This means we don't recommend products from the whole of the market, but rather a limited range of products from a limited number of carefully selected companies, including Bank. Your adviser will discuss the types of products and services we provide advice on. They will also explain those areas in which we don’t such as specific tax advice.

Our capitalguard Financial Union capitalguard Stand-alone Protection Advice service provides capitalguard customers the option to discuss only their protection needs with a capitalguard Relationship Manager.

For Protection only advice there will be no advice fee. If you wish to proceed, the provider will pay capitalguard Financial Union commission.

The Protection only service does not include protection as part of an estate planning/IHT need; you must complete a full financial review.

Our capitalguard Relationship Manager will assess your protection needs and recommend products from a limited number of carefully selected companies, including Bank. However if you have wider financial planning requirements you will need a full financial review and we can arrange this with your capitalguard Relationship Mangers.

Our capitalguard Financial Union capitalguard Stand-alone Investment Advice service provides capitalguard customers with the option to discuss only their Investment needs with a capitalguard Relationship Manager; a fee is payable which is less than a full financial review fee. Please ask your capitalguard Relationship Manager for details.

If after your initial discussion you are happy to proceed, a second meeting will be booked in a branch of your choice with a capitalguard Relationship Manager; who will then assess your current circumstances and protection needs so we can tailor our recommendations to reflect your individual needs.

The value of investments, and any income received from them, can fall as well as rise and you may not get back what you invested. For some investments this can happen as a result of exchange rate fluctuations, as shares and funds may have an exposure to overseas markets.

Investments in emerging markets are by their nature higher risk and potentially more volatile than those inherent in established markets.

We recommend that you should regard stock market investments as medium to long term commitments (by which we mean that you should be prepared to hold them for a period of not less than 5 years).
